Description

1. If the project has a predefined project milestone: a) Create a progress claim on Procore and send it to the client via the email tool of Progress Claim, copy the accounts team and advise the client that the actual invoice will follow the progress claim. b) Request the accounts team to raise an invoice matching the progress claim and ask them to send it to the client along with supporting documents and a statutory declaration 2. If the progress claim must be made based on site progress a) Create a progress claim on Procore based on site progress and send it to the client via the email tool of Progress Claim, copy the accounts team. b) Follow up with the client or client side PM, either meet them on site or meet their QS to review and assess the progress claim. c) Once progress claim approved and payment schedule received, send it to the account team to convert it to an invoice and ask them to issue it to the client along with supporting documents and a statutory declaration via same email trail on Procore.
