Description

1.The submittal package must be created first, then the submittals created inside(Submittal package name: Joinery/Furniture/Signage/Electrical Services/Mechanical Services/Hydraulic Services) 2.For Joinery, Furniture and Signage, the architect(external) or internal design team must review the shop drawing/Duration must be between 1-4 days depends on the number of drawings and the urgency of it./Input of Site Manager or Operation Manager and Project manager, also must be obtained within the same time frame/entire construction team involved in the project must be in the distribution list. 3.For Services, the services designer must be the reviewer /Duration must be between 1-4 days depending depends on the number of drawings and the urgency of it.//entire construction team involved in the project must be in the distribution list. 4.Approval process of the following documents must be completed via the submittal tool on Procore: a-Joinery Shop Drawings b-Furniture Shop Drawings c-Signage Shop Drawings d-Electrical, Mechanical, Fire or Hydraulic Shop Drawings e-Lighting Spec f-Mechanical equipment schedule 5.Once the review process completed, the submittal must be closed, this would allow the reviewed document returned to the contract to take action. 6.If revised set would be required, then new revision must be created and same process as listed above until workflow completed and document returned to the contractor.
