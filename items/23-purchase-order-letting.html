Description

Once confirmation is received from trades that their quote is aligned with the scope of works and the latest set of drawings and building rules, and the final price has been agreed on, the Subcontractor Agreement or Purchase order can be drafted on Procore. Ensuring all items related to the contract and PO have been determined and attached: 1. Payment Terms and include any deposit requirements 2. Default retention 3. Contract dates 4. Main Contractor’s Representative: 5. Site Contact: 6. Working Hours on Site 7. Insurances Required 8. Payment Term 9. Defects Liability Period 10. Warranties and Other Documents Required on Completion: 11. ANNEXURE A – Scope of Works. 12. The amount of Liquidated Damages per day (Clause 27) 13. Transmittal 14. ANNEXURE H - House Rules 15. ANNEXURE D – Program 16. ANNEXURE E – Drawings-Architectural Documents: 17. ANNEXURE F – Drawings-Engineer Documents: 18. ANNEXURE G – Fixtures & Finishes Documents: 19. ANNEXURE B – WHS Site Management Plan Once the above steps are completed, the subcontractor agreement can be reviewed and formally sent to the trades via email toll of commitment.
