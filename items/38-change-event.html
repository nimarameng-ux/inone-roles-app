Description

1. First step is filling the Excel spreadsheet which the template can be found on the server: - P:\InOne Projects\Active Projects\2X_XXX -XXX\05_In Construction\02_Variations\Variation working out template 2. The excel spreadsheet is usually based on the price that must be provided and shown to the client, so PM or CA can play with margins to make up the final number within the hidden column. 3. Change event has drop down the section regarding Type, Change Reason and Scope, and when filling the change event, must ensure the correct reason has been selected: 4. Type:(Client change, contingency, site condition, TBD or transfer)/Change reason:(Back charge, client request, Design Development, Existing Condition or Internal Variation), and if it is not recoverable, cost from client must be noted as Internal Variations and Scope must be determined if it is Out of Scope or In scope), TBD should not be used. 5. In the Line items section, you must ensure that at least one budget code for each trade(it can be more) has been entered. this will be handy after approval of HCV as it can be sent to the trade as well. 6. Inone margin must be entered as a separate line item, and it can hold the revealed part of the margin as per the agreed percentage under the contract. 7. Line items has Revenue section and the Cost section. Revenue must be filled with numbers that the client should see, and cost must be filled with trade cost. 8. Change event should not be shared with the trade or the client, it is the basis to start the Head Contract Variation or Subcontractor Variation off it.
